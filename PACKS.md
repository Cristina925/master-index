# PACKS — What to Pull When a Trigger Occurs

This document maps real-world triggers to concrete artefact groupings.
It is designed for use during regulatory inquiry, audit preparation,
incident response, or internal governance review.

---

## Pack 1: Decision Legitimacy & Continuity

**Use when**
- A delegated, automated, or AI-mediated action is questioned
- Authority or responsibility must be demonstrated at the time of action

**Typical questions**
- “Who was authorised to let this proceed?”
- “Was authority valid at the moment of execution?”
- “How was decision context preserved?”

**Typically includes**
- Decision context / decision continuity records
- Authority validity and revalidation artefacts
- Incident-safe language for internal and external use

---

## Pack 2: Record-Keeping & Transparency Readiness

**Use when**
- Regulators or auditors request evidence, not narrative
- Transparency or reporting duties apply

**Typical questions**
- “Show us the record.”
- “What evidence existed before execution?”
- “Who held responsibility and how is that captured?”

**Typically includes**
- Record-keeping readiness material
- Conformance records / responsible individual verification
- Evidence mapping artefacts

---

## Pack 3: Incident & Adverse Event Readiness

**Use when**
- An incident, harm event, or breach occurs
- Reporting timelines and preservation obligations activate

**Typical questions**
- “Is this reportable?”
- “What must be preserved immediately?”
- “What language is safe to use?”

**Typically includes**
- Incident and adverse event readiness artefacts
- Reporting timelines and preservation checklists
- Incident language material

---

## Pack 4: Online Safety & Platform Risk Readiness (UK / EU)

**Use when**
- Online safety risk assessment or transparency duties activate
- Enforcement exposure requires documented process

**Typical questions**
- “Where is the risk assessment record?”
- “How were safeguards governed and reviewed?”
- “What documentation supports compliance?”

**Typically includes**
- Online safety record-keeping readiness material
- Governance language artefacts
- Evidence mapping documents

---

## Pack 5: Sector-Specific Compliance Evidence

**Use when**
- Statutory product or sector obligations apply
- Fixed reporting or evidence deadlines exist

**Typical questions**
- “What evidence is required?”
- “What is the reporting clock?”
- “What must be included — and avoided?”

**Typically includes**
- Sector compliance evidence packs
- Adverse event or incident reporting artefacts
- Incident language guidance

---

## Design Principle

These packs are intentionally scoped.
They are designed to support **readiness**, **evidence**, and **defensibility**
without prescribing implementation or policy.
